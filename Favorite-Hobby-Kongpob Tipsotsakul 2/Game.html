>> GAMING PHILOSOPHY

SKILL (not) OVER LUCK

I go toward games where mechanical skill and game sense matter. Some RNG, no pay-to-win, Some shortcuts. Just luck or skills.

PRECISION IS EVERYTHING

Whether it's landing headshots in CSGO or executing silent takedowns in MGS, precision is the name of the game. One shot, one opportunity. Make it count.

OUTTHINK YOUR OPPONENT

Aim only takes you so far. Map knowledge, positioning, timing, psychology‚Äîthese separate good players from great ones. Always be three steps ahead.

NEVER SURRENDER

Down 0-12? Outnumbered 1v5? Low HP with no armor? Doesn't matter. Every match is winnable until the scoreboard says otherwise. Clutch or kick. That's what the teammates say.
