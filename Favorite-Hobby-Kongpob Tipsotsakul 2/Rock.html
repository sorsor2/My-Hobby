Evolution of rock music from 1970s to 2000s
Decade The Rock Revolution Iconic Moments
1970s Birth of hard rock and heavy metal Led Zeppelin IV, Dark Side of the Moon, Hard rock dominance
1980s Arena rock, hair metal, and MTV AC/DC's Back in Black, Guns N' Roses' Appetite for Destruction
1990s Grunge explosion and alternative rock Nirvana, Pearl Jam, the death of hair metal
2000s Rock's last stand and revival attempts The Strokes, The White Stripes, garage rock revival
